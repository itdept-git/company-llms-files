# Intelligent Product Solutions (IPS)

> IPS is a full-service product design and development company headquartered in Hauppauge, NY. We provide end-to-end engineering services from concept through production, specializing in medical devices, industrial IoT, wearables, hazardous environment products, and enterprise solutions. Founded in 2007 by former Symbol Technologies executives, IPS serves clients across North America and global markets.

## Company Overview

IPS offers comprehensive product development services including industrial design, mechanical engineering, embedded systems, software development, electrical engineering, systems architecture, optical engineering, UX/UI design, manufacturing services, and supply chain management. Our team of 75+ designers and engineers delivers market-ready, user-focused, and regulatory-compliant solutions.

Key differentiators:
- True end-to-end capabilities under one roof
- Holistic approach considering manufacturability, scalability, cost optimization, and time to market
- Co-located hardware and software teams enabling rapid collaboration
- Deep manufacturing expertise from founders' Symbol Technologies background
- Subsidiary of Forward Industries (NASDAQ: FORD)

**Corporate Structure:**
- IPS is a Forward Industries company (NASDAQ: FORD)
- Intelligent CloudCare (https://www.intelligentcloudcare.com) is a division of IPS, providing managed IT services and cybersecurity solutions
