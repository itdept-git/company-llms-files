# Intelligent CloudCare

> Intelligent CloudCare is a division of Intelligent Product Solutions (IPS), a Forward Industries company (NASDAQ: FORD). We are a managed IT services and cybersecurity provider serving small to mid-sized businesses and K-12 school districts in Long Island, NYC, and beyond. We deliver proactive IT support, cybersecurity solutions, and compliance guidance through our ADITA™ framework. As a Nassau BOCES selected IT and cybersecurity service provider, we specialize in helping organizations protect their data and infrastructure.

## Company Overview

CloudCare takes a multi-disciplinary approach to IT, combining managed IT services, cybersecurity, and software development under one umbrella. Our team holds certifications including Azure Security Engineer, CompTIA A+/Security+/Network+, AWS Cloud Practitioner, Certified Ethical Hacker, and Microsoft Solutions Expert.

## ADITA™ Framework

Our proprietary security framework guides organizations through:
- **Assess** current security posture
- **Define** compliant policies and practices
- **Implement** organizational changes
- **Train** teams to modify behavior
- **re-Assess** security posture periodically
